After a mishap that got you exiled from your nest, YOU; a cheeky squirrel find a crate full of acorns lying around, so you hop in and eat it all!

Unexpectedly, you wash up on a mountainous island in Australia.

You meet a local koala who wants to reach a giant acorn atop the mountain, so you team up and journey towards the summit together.

Use your unique squirrel abilities to jump, climb, and roll your way to the mountain's summit, finding delicious acorn snacks along the way!
